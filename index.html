About Our Village

Poocholamadu is a picturesque village nestled in the heart of nature, known for its rich cultural heritage and warm community.

Founded centuries ago, our village has maintained its traditional values while embracing progress. The name "Poocholamadu" reflects our deep connection with the land and our ancestors.

With lush green fields, ancient temples, and a close-knit community, Poocholamadu offers a glimpse into authentic rural life that's becoming rare in today's fast-paced world.
